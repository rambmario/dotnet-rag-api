Lexa Legal is a law firm and real estate consultancy based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Its main services include estate succession, property purchase and sale transactions, and contract drafting.

The firm also works with process automation and artificial intelligence tools applied to legal documents and client management.

Client attention is focused on providing clarity, proximity, and legal certainty throughout every process.

Lexa Legal operates with a team of experienced lawyers and real estate specialists, offering personalized advice to individuals and companies across Argentina and internationally.
